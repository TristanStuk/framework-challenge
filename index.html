"Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" is a sequel to "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" and takes Miles Morales on an adventure through the multiverse. On this adventure you'll meet new spider-people and watch as Miles overcomes incredible challenges in this visual achievement that is an animated movie.
